The faces of the pyramid had been built at a steep angle and it has been suggested that this was to ensure the bodies of sacrificed people rolled to the bottom of the structure once their hearts had been removed at the top. There were two parallel staircases up one side of the temple leading to the temple platform and the two shrines of Tlaloc and Huitzilopochtli.
