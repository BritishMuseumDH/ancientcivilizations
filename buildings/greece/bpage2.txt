tex=The Greeks worshipped many gods.  Twelve gods were thought to be the most important; Zeus, Hera, Athena, Apollo, Artemis, Poseidon, Aphrodite, Hermes, Hephaistos, Ares, Demeter and Dionysos.  However, there were many other lesser gods who still needed to be worshipped.  Each god could take many forms, Athena, the patron goddess of Athens could be the goddess of War, Victory or Wisdom at different times in different places.
