Explore the city

As with a modern city, the buildings in Constantinople fulfilled different purposes. Of course there were houses for people to live in, but there were also buildings which were not for domestic use. These can be grouped by similar functions, for example: religious buildings such as a church or temple; civic buildings such as a market or city hall; government buildings such as a palace or council building and entertainment buildings such as a theatre or public bath.

You can now choose from several themed trails around the city. Each trail will visit buildings that broadly share a particular purpose
