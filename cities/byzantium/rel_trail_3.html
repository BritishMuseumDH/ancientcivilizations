The Church of SS Sergius and Bacchus

This church, dedicated to two martyr saints, was built by Justinian in the 530s, when Hagia Sophia was also built. It was the centre for Monophysite worship, which was a branch of Christianity that disagreed with some of the ideas about Jesus held by the main body of the Church.
