&tex=<font face="Arial" color="#FFFFFF" size="14">During the second millennium BC, large <font color="#FFCC66"><a href="javascript:openGlos('glossary/s/settlement.html','glos')"><u>settlements</u></a></font> started to develop on the island of Crete and on the Greek mainland.  People started to live together in larger groups and urban life began. However, not all of the settlements developed in the same way.  
Two of the most important cities, Mycenae, on mainland Greece and Knossos, on Crete, had many similarities, but some important differences.  These differences were partly due to the terrain and partly due to the lifestyle and people who inhabited the sites.   </font face>
