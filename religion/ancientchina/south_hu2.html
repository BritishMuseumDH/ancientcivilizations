Cormorant fishing

Detail from the hu - a cormorant fishing
is a traditional way of fishing in China. The fisherman goes out on his boat at dusk and takes his trained cormorants with him. He puts a torch on the front of the boat to attract the fish to the surface. The cormorants scoop the fish from the water and return to the boat. They have a collar around their neck to prevent them swallowing the fish. So, the bird spits the fish out for the fisherman.
