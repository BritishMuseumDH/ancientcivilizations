The Belt Buckle
This is a very large gold buckle that weighs over 400g. The decoration uses intricate animal and bird-based designs. The buckle can be opened, so could have contained objects, such as Christian relics. Like the purse lid, experts think that the object was made in England by a master craftsman, as the object displays a very high standard of workmanship. Being able to commission such objects shows that the king was very wealthy.
