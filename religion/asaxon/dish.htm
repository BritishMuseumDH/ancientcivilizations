The Anastasius Dish
There were sixteen pieces of silver buried in the grave and this dish is the largest of them all. It has a diameter of 72.4 cm and was made from one lump of silver! The dish has been impressed with a stamp of Emperor Anastasius I, who ruled between AD 491 and 518, so the dish was already very old by the time it was placed in the burial. Like the other silverware, this object came from the Byzantine Empire.
