The Helmet
The helmet, when found, was in hundreds of pieces and has been carefully reconstructed. It was made of iron but was decorated with embossed bronze sheeting coated with tin, which would have given it a silvery appearance. The replica shows how impressive the helmet would have been when made. The decoration is Swedish in style and the bronze plates may have been made by Swedish craftsmen. The helmet shows the warrior status of the man who was buried.
