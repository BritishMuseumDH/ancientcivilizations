The Purse Lid
The purse lid is made of a kidney-shaped piece of whale bone surrounded by a gold frame, with seven decorative gold plaques set with garnets and millefiori glass. The actual purse has disintegrated but was probably a pouch-like bag that hung below the lid. It is a beautifully made object and experts think it was probably made by the king's own jewellers. The fact that he had master craftsmen working for him using precious materials demonstrates the wealth of the king.
