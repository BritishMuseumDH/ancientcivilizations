The Shield
This was in pieces when found, like the helmet, and has been reconstructed. The shield would have been made of limewood covered with animal hide. There was an iron boss in the middle. This was probably intended for display purposes, not for actual fighting, as it was made of rich materials, some of which are quite fragile. Like the helmet, it was probably made by Swedish armourers or perhaps in Sweden. The shield also displayed the warrior status of the man, who had a complete set of military equipment buried with him. This emphasised his royalty.
