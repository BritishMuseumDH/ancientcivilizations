Find out about Akapana as a religious building

On the top of the terraces were living quarters which are thought to have belonged to priests that carried out the rituals on the summit of Akapana. Burials were found beneath the patio of the living quarters.
A line of adults was found facing a seated male who was holding an incense burner. Originally, these bodies would have been wrapped in textile bundles. These burials are thought to belong to members of an elite in Tiwanaku.
