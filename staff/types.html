Types of Resource

Scheme of work:

The scheme shows one way to use the www.earlyimperialchina.co.uk website to facilitate the history programme of study for Key Stage 3. Use as much or as little of the scheme as you wish.

Lessons:

These give a plan for each lesson, as detailed in the scheme of work.

Background information sheets:

We have included a wide range of general information which teachers may find helpful.

Worksheets:

These contain handouts for classroom activities. Where needed the worksheets may contain notes for teachers. Some of the worksheets are linked to the lesson plans included in the scheme of work.
