tex=Paper making was essential in the invention of printing and this too was a Chinese invention.  Paper-making was perfected in China in AD 105 by an imperial official called Cai Lun.<br><br>Previously texts had been written on wooden or bamboo strips or silk. Paper, however, was much lighter, cheaper, and more portable. <br><br>Paper was made from hemp, bamboo or tree bark, these were firstly soaked in water.  The soaking material would then be boiled and pounded until it had formed a pulp.  A screen was dipped in and out of this pulp and would pick up a thin layer of fibres.  While the screen dried so did the fibres. They would join together to form a sheet of paper.
